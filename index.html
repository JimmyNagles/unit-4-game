Crystals Collector!

Instructions

you will be given a random number.

there are four crystals with diferent values and by clicking a crystal you will add a specific amount to the initial score.

you win the game by matching the random number and your score.

if you dont match the random number and your score you lose.

each time the game re-starts the values of the crystals change.
